Homework #2 - Due July 2nd

Find a data visualization in the world around you. This can be something you see on a webpage or email, in a book or magazine, on a billboard or advertisement, or anywhere else.

Please come to class #2 ready to discuss:

  • What is this data visualization trying to communicate?
  • What is the underlying data?
  • How effective do you find it? How clear is the presentation?
  • How else could the designer have communicated the same information?

Take a screenshot or picture of the data visualization and send it to me by email, so we can discuss as a group.
