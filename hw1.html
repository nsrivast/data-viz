Homework #1

The first homework is meant to help us get to know each other in the first class. It will also get you started in thinking about data!

Please come to class #1 with a piece of numerical data that describes something about your background, your life, or your interests. This assignment is asking you to quantify some aspect of your personal life.

Some basic examples might be:

  • the number of books, or sneakers, or T-shirts you own
  • the time (in days) since you graduated university
  • the price (in shillings) of your favorite meal at your favorite restaurant
  • the distance (in kilometers) you live from your birth place
  • the win/loss record of your favorite football team

Pick something that tells people something about yourself, and is unique to you!
