This course presents the principles and techniques of effective data visualization. Students learn fundamental concepts in communicating information through graphics and apply these concepts to build visualizations of their own.

So far, I have taught this course to two different groups of software developers and data scientists in Nairobi during the summer of 2015. I also plan to offer a similar curriculum at programs in New York and Philadelphia through 2017.

One Day Seminar: iHub

This material was presented in a seminar at the Summer Data Jam at the iHub, a coworking space and incubator in Nairobi.

Part-Time Course: Moringa School

This curriculum was presented over three weeks to a group of software developers at Moringa School, a programming bootcamp in Nairobi.
